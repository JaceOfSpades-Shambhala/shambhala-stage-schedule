    Release this Hexlace?

    Your name, shared list, and Hex Owl come off this physical tag so someone else can claim it. Your saved sets and Hex Owl stay with your profile, ready if you reclaim a Hexlace. This needs internet and can't be undone.

    Trade Hexlaces with a friend

    Both people should open this option. Then each person taps the other person's physical Hexlace to their own phone.
