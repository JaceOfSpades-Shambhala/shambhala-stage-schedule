How it works

Built for the moment, not the scroll

1One permanent key

The key stays with the physical hexlace through releases, reclaims, name changes, and new phones.

2One cached source mark

The supplied Shambhala Owl vector is loaded once. Lightweight SVG overlays provide the variation offline.

3Trade in person

A confirmed physical hexlace trade moves its Hex Owl too. The fun is the exchange; then the phone goes away.

Trait system

Accessories and room to grow

The mockup’s extra ideas are included without changing the original Owl anatomy.

ActiveFestival accessories

Single and double kandi, LED ear cuffs, glow hoops, a disco chin gem, and glowstick earrings stay clear of the Owl edges.

ActiveExact-vector brow gems

Crown Gem, Brow Echo, Triple Prism, and Moonstone recolour portions of the original brow path instead of drawing feathers over it.

ActiveFacial disc patterns

UV glow, freckles, arcs, and diamond dust stay inside the open cheek pockets below the original eyes.

ActiveGem beaks

The original mark remains most common, with small amber, moonstone, and laser-prism shards fitted into the centre opening.

ActivePortal finish and rarity

Nearly every Owl uses one portal colour. Pagoda Sunset, Aurora Bloom, and Moon Prism are reserved for legendary Owls and future camp-only keys.

ActiveSubtle aura

Quiet, short portal rays, or sparse festival confetti for ordinary Owls; rare and legendary Owls receive the sparkle field.

ActiveYear stamp

A subtle 26 remembers when this first trait pack hatched while later packs can add new editions.

Held for campCamp-only Owls

A future reserved key range can identify Owls handed out in person; ordinary random keys never receive it.
